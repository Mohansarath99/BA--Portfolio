## Featured Project — On-Premise to Cloud CRM Migration

**Client:** Home Equity Bank, Toronto
**Role:** Senior Business Analyst
**Duration:** Sep 2025 – Present
**Tech Stack:** Salesforce, SQL, Power BI, JIRA, Confluence

### Project Summary
Led end-to-end business analysis for the migration of a legacy on-premise customer database to a cloud-based Salesforce CRM. The project involved profiling 50,000+ customer records, defining migration rules, coordinating UAT across 3 business units, and achieving go-live with a 96.2% data quality score.

### Project Outcomes
- Migrated 50,000+ customer records with 96.2% data quality score
- Reduced lead response time from 48–72 hours to under 2 hours
- Improved lead conversion rate from 12% to 19.4%
- Eliminated 4 hours/week of manual reporting per manager
- Delivered on time across 5 sprints with zero critical defects at go-live
